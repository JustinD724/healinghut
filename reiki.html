~How is a Reiki session performed?~

            Reiki sessions can be performed in a multiple of ways, But it should be noted that Reiki isn't a form of massage (in a physical sense).
            For those who may feel uncomfortable being touched, Reiki is performed by using the hands, which are hovering over your body. No physical touching is needed unless requested by you. The Reiki performer or guide uses intense focus and meditation to transfer energy to and from his or her body into yours.
            A Reiki session can be performed by laying down on a comfortable bed, or by sitting in a relaxed position. For best results, Laying down is the most popular way to have a Reiki session done. It allows the instructor or performer to have full control over healing your body, or root cause of your discomforts.
